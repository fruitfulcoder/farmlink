What is Farm Link ?

Farmlink is a company based in Lexington, Massachusetts which specializes in bringing all the extra food that is rotting in farms to hungry customers who can't get fresh produce from the empty grocery stores, and at the same time helps farmers make money off what they would normally be thrown away
